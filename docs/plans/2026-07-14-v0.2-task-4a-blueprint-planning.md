## Acceptance criteria

- The Launchpad opens the real intake and example prompts populate the composer.
- A complete idea may proceed directly to Blueprint Review; an incomplete idea may receive at most three focused questions on one screen.
- Valid blueprints use `top_down_arena`, contain three to five acyclic quests, and contain only safe creator-facing planning data.
- Absolute paths, commands, packages/dependencies, arbitrary file content, workflow claims, unsupported foundations, invalid references, and missing verification links fail validation.
- The runtime makes no silent model substitution and performs at most one structure-repair turn.
- Blueprint approval changes only in-memory planning state; it writes no project files and runs no project/Godot commands.
- Review and Ready screens expose truthful provenance and keep technical detail behind disclosure.
- The real sample path, default launch, v0.1 legacy launch, and protected compatibility suite remain unchanged.
- Focused, full, production, browser, reduced-motion, and real GPT-5.6 rehearsal evidence pass before closeout.
